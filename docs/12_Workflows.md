# 12 Workflows

## Sales Process (Order to Cash)
1. **Quotation:** Non-binding offer to customer.
2. **Sales Order:** Confirmed request from customer. (Reduces available stock, but not physical stock).
3. **Delivery Note:** Physical movement of goods. (Reduces physical stock).
4. **Sales Invoice:** Financial claim. (Posts to Ledger).
5. **Customer Receipt:** Payment collection. (Clears AR).

## Purchase Process (Procure to Pay)
1. **Purchase Requisition:** Internal request.
2. **Purchase Order (PO):** Formal order to supplier.
3. **Goods Received Note (GRN):** Physical receipt. (Increases physical stock).
4. **Supplier Bill:** Financial obligation. (Posts to Ledger).
5. **Supplier Payment:** Settlement. (Clears AP).

## Inventory Adjustment Workflow
1. **Count:** Physical inventory check.
2. **Adjustment Entry:** Documenting difference.
3. **Approval:** Manager review of variance.
4. **Posting:** Updates stock and posts to "Inventory Variance" expense account.

## Expense Approval Workflow
1. **Employee Submission:** Attachment of receipt.
2. **Manager Review:** Verify against budget.
3. **Accounting Verification:** Correct account coding.
4. **Payment:** Reimbursing employee or paying vendor.
