# 25 Future Features

## Intelligent Automation
- **AI Assistant:** "Show me my top 5 customers who are late on payments."
- **OCR Receipt Recognition:** Automatically create expense entries from photos.
- **Auto-Categorization:** AI suggests account codes based on vendor name.

## Financial Expansion
- **Bank Integration:** Plaid/Salt Edge integration for real-time bank feeds.
- **Multi-Company Consolidation:** One-click consolidated P&L and Balance Sheet for groups.
- **Budgeting & Forecasting:** Predictive cash flow based on historical data.

## Module Additions
- **Manufacturing (Advanced):** Bill of Materials, Work Orders, and Cost of Production tracking.
- **Fixed Assets:** Automatic depreciation schedules and asset tracking.
- **Projects:** Job costing, timesheet tracking, and project-based invoicing.
- **POS (Point of Sale):** Offline-capable retail interface with barcode support.

## Ecosystem
- **Customer Portal:** Customers can view invoices and pay online.
- **Supplier Portal:** Suppliers can upload bills and view payment status.
- **Employee Self-Service:** Apply for leave, view payslips, and claim expenses.
- **Open API:** Allow third-party apps to sync with LedGix.

## Compliance
- **XBRL Reporting:** Support for standardized financial reporting formats.
- **Advanced Audit Trail:** Comparison tool to see exactly what changed between two versions of a document.
