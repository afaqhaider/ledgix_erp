# 01 Product Strategy

## Why LedGix ERP Exists
Many SMEs are stuck between using spreadsheets, which are prone to error and lack auditability, and traditional ERPs, which are prohibitively expensive and difficult to implement. LedGix ERP exists to provide a middle ground: professional ERP features at a fraction of the cost and complexity.

## Problems Solved
- **Data Silos:** Bringing sales, inventory, accounting, and HR into one source of truth.
- **Human Error:** Automating double-entry accounting to prevent manual mistakes.
- **Lack of Visibility:** Real-time reporting for owners who are often away from the office.
- **Compliance Hurdles:** Simplifying VAT and financial reporting for tax compliance.

## Competitors
- **QuickBooks Online / Xero:** Strong on accounting, but often lack deep ERP modules (Manufacturing, Advanced Inventory).
- **Odoo:** Powerful but can be complex and expensive when customized.
- **ERPNext:** Robust but often requires technical expertise to host and manage.
- **Local Legacy Software:** Desktop-based, lacks cloud features and modern UI.

## Unique Advantages
- **Flutter-First:** A truly unified experience across Web, Android, and iOS.
- **Firebase Backend:** Real-time updates and world-class scalability out of the box.
- **Accountant-Friendly:** Designed by accountants for businesses, ensuring the ledger is always clean.
- **Pure Black Dark Mode:** A focus on modern aesthetics and reduced eye strain for power users.

## Long-Term Direction
1. **Foundation (Current):** Build robust core modules (Accounting, Sales, Purchase, Inventory).
2. **Expansion:** Introduce complex modules (Manufacturing, Projects, HR/Payroll).
3. **Intelligence:** Implement AI for OCR receipt recognition and predictive cash flow.
4. **Ecosystem:** Open APIs and marketplace for third-party integrations.
5. **Enterprise:** Shift to dedicated PostgreSQL/Microservices architecture for massive scale.
